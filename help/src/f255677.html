Generates values to be used when sorting in a non-standard way.

sortkey (char_expression [, {collation_name | collation_ID}])

Parameter information :
»char_expression : A variable, constant or column of datatype char or varchar (null or not null) or of unichar or univarchar.
»collation_name : The name of a collation sequence.
»collation_ID : The internal ID of type integer of a collation sequence.
