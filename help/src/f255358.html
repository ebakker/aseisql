Returns the angle in radians of the specified sine and cosine of an angle.

atn2(sine, cosine)

Parameter information :
»sine : A constant, column or variable of datatype float, real or double precision, or a datatype that can implicitly be converted into one of these.
»cosine : A constant, column or variable of datatype float, real or double precision, or a datatype that can implicitly be converted into one of these.
