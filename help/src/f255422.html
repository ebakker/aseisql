Shows a part of a (small)datetime expression as an integer value.

datepart(date_part, date_expression)

Parameter information :
»date_part : A portion of a date. Possible values: caldayofweek, calweekofyear, calyearofweek, cdw : Day of the calendarweek, cwk : Week of a calendar year, cyr : Year of the calendar week., day, dayofyear, dd : Day, dw : Weekday, dy : Day of year, hh : Hour, hour, mi : Minute, millisecond, minute, mm : Month, month, ms : Millisecond, qq : Quarter, quarter, second, ss : Second, week, weekday, wk : Week, year, yy : Year
»date_expression : An expression of the (small)datetime or a value that can implicitly be converted into a datetime value.
